Three-fund portfolio calculator

This WebApp helps computing investment amounts to the base three indices.

In the book The Bogleheads' Guide to the Three-Fund Portfolio authors recommend investing in three main index funds.

We want funds to be diverse, uncorrelated and have minimal fee.

Authors propose to use:

  • VBTLX: Vanguard Total Bond Market Index Fund Admiral Shares
  • VTSAX: Vanguard Total Stock Market Index Fund Admiral Shares
  • VTIAX: Vanguard Total International Stock Index Fund Admiral Shares

or similar.

The do not tell what should be the ratio of the portfolio in each fund, but suggest the following numbers as a baseline:

  • VBTLX: Your age.
  • VTSAX: 100% - VTIAX% - (your age)%
  • VTIAX: 20%

You can use the above values, or decrease bond component if you are less risk averse and increase if you are.
